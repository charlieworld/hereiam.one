FAQ

FAQ

Q: How is this different to the life extension service provided by Alc*r Life Extension Foundation?

A: Alc*r provides long term low temperature preservation of human bodies. It is totally different to our cross-species genetic transformation services.

Q: How is it different from human cloning.

A: At the moment, there is no company or lab which holds the technology to clone human. The core technology of HEREiAM™ includes only mature techniques. Before providing any service, we have evaluated the stability of the service. What we do is transferring your gene to another (non-animal) organism for reproduction. It is not human cloning.

Q: What will happen to the GMO which has my genes?

A: Our company does not express your genes in other organism. That is, the GMO will carry the fragment of our gene but your gene will not have any effect on the GMO. This is the most efficient and environmental friendly approach.

Q: Can I put more-than-one piece of gene into the GMO?

A: Of course you can. If you are interested in doing so, please contact our product specialists.

Q: Can I transfer my gene into another human by genetic transformation?

A: Due to the risk and controversy of human experiments, as well as the limitation of current technologies, our company does not offer such services.
